Blackjack
To start playing, select an amount of chips to wager, then confirm wager.
You can hit to draw another card, or stay to end the game.
If you score less than the dealer, you lose. If you score more than 21, you lose.
If you score more than the dealer, you win. Amount of chips won scales with your wager.

0+ wagered: X1.5 chips
100+ wagered: X1.75 chips
200+ wagered: X2 chips
500+ wagered: X3 chips
